Akan Names

Derived from the Ghanian Culture, Akan names are names given to children which corresponds to the day of the week that they were born and order in which they were born. Read more

This website works in a way that after you input you date of birth and gender, it generates results and returns to you your Ghanian name, which ofcourse depends on the specific day of the week you were born in.
